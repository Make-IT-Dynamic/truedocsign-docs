Data Deletion

How to Delete Your TruedocSign Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, follow these instructions:

Access your profile on the website.

Click “Delete Account.”

Response Time: Up to 30 days after the request.

What Will Be Deleted: All user data, including name, email, and photograph, as well as signature and invoice history.

Notice: Some data may be retained due to legal obligations, for example, financial invoice records if required by law.
